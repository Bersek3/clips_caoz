How to download a Kick clip?

Step 1: Get clip URL
On the Kick.com website, search for the clip you wish to download, press the share button, and then copy the clip's URL.

Step 2: Paste clip URL
Simply paste the URL into the designated text box on Kickclips, and proceed by clicking the "Download" button.

Step 3: Download MP4 clip
After obtaining the video, you can easily download it by simply right-clicking on the displayed video and selecting "Save video as" or, click on the "Save File" button to initiate the direct download.
